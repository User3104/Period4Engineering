Week Ten: 10/24/2022 In our tenth week, we are moving into the feild of environmental engineering. We learned how water is proccessed and treated at a facility in order to prevent damage to the environement. We learned that water filtration is a multi stage process involving varius types of filters, temperatures, pressures, and tecniques. We then went on to build a water filter out of some given materals in order to filter some dirty water that was provided by our teacher. Some designs worked better than other at filtering the dirty water due to the order of the filtering materials that was put into the filter.
